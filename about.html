About Content Director

My Journey

Over ten years working at the intersection of brand, content, and culture, across international agencies and fast-growing startups. I've led communications strategies for consumer brands, media companies, and Web3 projects across Europe, with the same focus: content that earns attention and builds genuine connection.

My career has taken me through VICE, PlayGround, SAE Institute, Danone, and ZIGChain, giving me a perspective that spans editorial, social media, and brand strategy, and a deep belief that the best content starts by understanding what people actually care about.
